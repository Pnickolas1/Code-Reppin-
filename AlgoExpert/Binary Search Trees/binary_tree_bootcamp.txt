


Full trees:
    - leaf nodes have no children
    - OR a node that has children, has 2 children (they have to have 2 children)


Complete trees:
    - complete trees are binary heaps
    - when we fill out the nodes, the nodes fall into place:
        - top to bottom
        - left to right


Perfect trees:
    - all leafs are on same level
    - all nodes that have descendent have 2 children
    - a perfect tree is full and also a complete tree


PreOrder:   Node | Leftsubtree | Rightsubtree

Inorder:    Leftsubtree | Node | Rightsubtree

Postorder:  Leftsubtree | Rightsubtree | Node
